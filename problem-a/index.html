Luna Lovegood

Luna Lovegood is from the popular series of Harry Potter by JK Rowling. Luna Lovegood is a very unique character. She has a funky sense of style and an airy, carefree personality. She is authentically herself and doesn't care what anyone else thinks.

What I admire about Luna Lovegood:

  • I admire her confidence and carefree spirit.
  • I admire her funky style.
  • I admire that she is a believer in all things weird and odd.
